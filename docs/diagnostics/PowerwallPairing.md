# Netzero - Powerwall Pairing

## Introduction

[Netzero](https://www.netzero.energy) pairing with your Powerwall enables advanced features like Go Off-Grid automations and Local Monitoring.

During pairing, Netzero registers a secure access key on the Powerwall so it can communicate directly with the device. You can remove this key at any time in either the Netzero or Tesla app.

Pairing is supported for both Powerwall 2 and Powerwall 3 systems. This feature requires a Netzero subscription.

## Pairing Process

You must be physically near the Powerwall to toggle its power switch during pairing. In Netzero, go to **Settings > Powerwall Pairing** and tap **Begin Pairing**. After pairing starts, you have about **two minutes** to toggle the Powerwall switch.

The switch location differs between Powerwall generations:

- **Powerwall 2**: Find the switch on the **right side** of the Powerwall. Toggle it **off** and then **back on**.
- **Powerwall 3**: Find the switch on the **left side** of the Powerwall. Flip the cover open, then toggle the switch **off** and then **back on**.

If you have more than one Powerwall, only **one** switch needs to be toggled.

## Pairing Status

After pairing is complete, the **Powerwall Pairing** screen shows **Paired** status with a green checkmark.

If something goes wrong, remove the pairing in either the Netzero or Tesla app and try again.

## Advanced Functionality

Once paired, you will see new automation actions under **Off-grid control**: **Go off-grid** and **Reconnect to the grid**. These can help with certain utility rate plans:
1. If your utility has **negative export pricing**, you can go off-grid to avoid export penalties. Reconnect to the grid once prices are no longer negative.
2. If your utility has **demand charges**, go off-grid to avoid imports during demand-charge windows. Reconnect to the grid once the window ends.

Future Netzero releases will also enable Local Monitoring for paired Powerwalls: more frequent dashboard energy-flow updates that continue even when internet access is down.
